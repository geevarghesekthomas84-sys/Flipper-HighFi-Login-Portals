# ETHICS.md - Security Research Charter

Ethical conduct is the foundation of professional security research. This project is provided as a high-fidelity template collection to study UI/UX replication and captive portal mechanics in a **laboratory or controlled hardware environment**.

## 📖 Rules of Engagement

1.  **Strictly Laboratory Use**: These templates should only be deployed on hardware that you own (e.g., your own Flipper Zero, ESP32, or local server).
2.  **Explicit Consent**: Never deploy these portals in a public environment where non-consenting individuals could connect to them. Security research should never interfere with the privacy or operations of others.
3.  **No Malicious Intent**: The exfiltration logic (Discord webhooks, etc.) is included as a primary demonstration of frontend-to-service data flows. It should not be used for unauthorized data collection.
4.  **Local Data Handling**: If you are conducting research, prioritize the **Local Storage** mode to ensure that test data remains entirely offline and within your control.
5.  **Educational Transparency**: If sharing your work, always clearly state that the portals are replicas for study purposes and provide links to official platform documentation.

## ⚖ Responsibility

By utilizing these templates, you agree to take full responsibility for your actions. The goal of this repository is to **build better defenders** by understanding the visual sophistication of modern social engineering studies.
